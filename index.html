How fast can AVA be live on our lines?
Standard deployments go live within 24 hours. Deposit in, intake submitted, clock starts. We port or forward your existing number — no hardware, no dispatcher downtime.
What happens when AVA can't answer something?
AVA warm-transfers to your dispatcher with full call context — caller, pickup, drop, vehicle class, rate quoted. Smart escalation, not dead ends.

200 simultaneous inbound calls with zero hold time. AVA scales with the crush — prom night, Super Bowl weekend, Friday 4:47 PM — without ever dropping a caller.
