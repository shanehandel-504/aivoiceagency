FAQ

Questions we actually get asked.

How fast can AVA be live?
Standard deployments go live within 24 hours. Deposit in, intake submitted, clock starts. We handle the entire technical build — you hand us the intake, we hand you a live system.
What happens when AVA can't answer something?
AVA transfers to a human with full call context. Smart escalation, not dead ends. You pick up knowing exactly what's happening.
Do I need special equipment?
No. AVA works with your existing phone number. We port or forward — zero hardware required on your end.
How much does it cost?
Starter plans begin at $497/month plus a one-time $500 setup fee. Pro and Enterprise tiers scale with features and call volume. Month-to-month — cancel anytime, no long-term contracts.
Can AVA handle multiple locations?
Yes. Enterprise and White Glove plans support multi-location routing, separate scripts per location, and unified reporting across all sites.
