FAQ

Questions we actually get asked.

How long does it take to get set up?
Live in 7–10 business days (faster for simple setups). A fully trained agent with your pricing, rules, and booking logic is production-ready in under two weeks. We handle the entire technical side — you answer a few questions about your business and we build it from there.
Will callers know it's AI?
The agent sounds natural, uses your business name, and follows your tone. Most callers don't notice. And if they ask, the system can be transparent about it — that's entirely your call. We set it up the way you want.
What if I already have a receptionist?
The agent handles overflow, after-hours, and high-volume periods. It doesn't replace your team — it makes sure nothing falls through the cracks. Your receptionist focuses on conversations that need a human. The agent covers the rest.
How much does it cost?
Custom build. Month-to-month. We price based on call volume and integrations — no long-term contracts. Book a discovery call and we'll scope it. If it's not working for you, you're not locked in.
What happens when something goes wrong?
Human quality control is built into the system. We monitor calls, review edge cases, and update the agent continuously. If something needs your attention, it escalates to you immediately. You're never flying blind.
What about compliance (HIPAA, TCPA, etc.)?
We scope compliance requirements during the discovery call. If your industry has specific regulations, we configure the agent accordingly — call recording disclosures, data handling, opt-in flows, all of it. Compliance is built into the setup, not bolted on after.
